GIVE THE ARKABA WALK AS A GIFT

We're inspired by the word "Tell me, what is it you plan to do with your one wild and precious life?"
MARY OLIVER

Particularly now, in pursuit of offering memorable gifts and sharing meaningful moments. Ones that may leave you a little dusty at times but won't gather dust on your bookshelf.

Our outback Australian landscape has inspired films, painters and pioneers for years. Give the gift of our award-winning Arkaba Walk and immerse yourself, your friends & family in a Wild Bush Luxury story. It’s an experience brimming with wild. Loaded with purpose. Wrapped in style.

Step out of your world and into our 60,000 acre wildlife conservancy in South Australia’s Flinders Ranges. Walk through Adnyamathanha land and take a bucket shower with the setting sun rolling down the rocky edge of Wilpena Pound. Gather fireside for a drop of wine and count stars from your swag on this four-day Arkaba Walk experience. This is a Great Walk of Australia. This is outback walking in style.

VALIDITY

All gift vouchers are non-refundable and valid for Arkaba Walk bookings made within 12 months of purchase.

VALUE

The value of your gift card can be of your choosing to be put towards a booking made by the recipient, or the equivalent to the four-day Wild Bush Luxury experience on the Arkaba Walk from Arkaba at $2,400 or $2,900 all-inclusive with return transfers from Adelaide. Prices are person twin-share and lucky recipients are welcome to upgrade to include transfers from Adelaide at time of booking if they wish. Find out about what’s included. 

GIVING BACK TO WILDLIFE

Your Arkaba Walk experience is more than a walking holiday. By joining us on your outback adventure you are actively contributing to our conservation mission to help save the biodiversity of Australia’s wildlife. Over five million native animals (and counting!) thank you from the bottom of their fluffy, feathery and scaly reptilian hearts.

DELIVERY

Vouchers can be sent electronically or by express post within Australia with our compliments. Please allow up to three working days by email and seven for gift vouchers by snail mail.
